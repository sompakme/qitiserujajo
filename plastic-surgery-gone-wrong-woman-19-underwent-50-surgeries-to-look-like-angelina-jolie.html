    We live in a society that often gives the perception that one must look a certain way to be labelled as ‘hot’. A society where it is okay for a 17-year-old girl to get lip fillers.

    The world had the Stone age, Bronze age, Iron age, and well, looks like we are now in the plastic age.

    A lot of people are opting to buy what they were not born with. Hence, the rise of plastic surgery.

    Although cosmetic surgery is often used to help people born with deformities, or help with breathing difficulties through rhinoplasty; the majority of people undergo the surgery purely for aesthetic reasons.

    People get so obsessed with the idea of how they should look, that they lose themselves completely in the process.

    Take Sahar Tabar for an example. The 19-year-old Iranian woman underwent at least 50 surgeries to look like her Idol, Angelina Jolie.

    Legit.ng gathered that the young female claims to be Jolie’s biggest fan, and would you to any length necessary to resemble the Tomb Raider actress.

    Unfortunately, the procedures went horribly wrong, leaving Tabar not resembling Jolie, or her old self.

    Apart from the surgery, Tabar shed half of her body weight, and now maintains the mere 40kg she weighs through a strict diet.

    However, Tabar seems to be happy as she posts pics of herself on Instagram to her 357k followers, despite people thinking she looks more like what is found in the tomb, than the Lara Croft actress that finds it.
