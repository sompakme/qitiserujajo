    Early Life and Education

    David Drew Pinsky was born on September 4, 1958, in Pasadena, California. His father is Morton Pinsky, a physician who worked as an assistant professor of psychiatry at the University of Southern California, and his mother is Helene Stanton, a renowned actress.
