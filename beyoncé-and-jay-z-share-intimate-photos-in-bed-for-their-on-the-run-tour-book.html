    24 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Beyoncé and JAY-Z continue to take over the world with their On the Run II tour, and the accompanying visuals are proving to be just as exciting as the actual performances. Last week, when the series of shows kicked off in Cardiff, Wales, fans were over the moon to find the Carters had included footage of their twins, Rumi and Sir, daughter Blue Ivy, and possible videos of their vow renewals.

    But the couple shared even more intimate images for their On the Run Tour Book, which some lucky fans were able to acquire at the concerts. The booklets include photos of Beyoncé and JAY-Z stripped down in bed.

    One shot features Bey in nothing but a yellow thong—in the middle of doing a newspaper crossword puzzle, mind you—while her rapper puffs away at a cigar beside her.

    In another snap, the pair cuddles up au naturel.

    Another picture in the never-before-seen set shows Bey wading into the ocean nude.

    As expected, fans freaked out over the photos.

    Love seems to be an overarching theme for the Carters' joint tour, considering the romantic moments from their marriage and sweet family footage that have appeared onscreen during their shows, accompanied by messages like, "This is real love" and "Love is universal."

    Beyoncé and JAY-Z don't bring the On the Run II tour to the U.S. until July 25. We can only imagine what surprises they'll have in store for us then.
