    The New York City rain has nothing on Irina Shayk, who not only braved the chilly precipitation yesterday, but even made it a cool, fashionable outing.

    The concrete jungle streets became the supermodel’s runway, as she stepped out in an outfit that was equal parts dainty and edgy. Shayk looked lovely in a long, flowy white slipdress that hit just above her ankles, layered with a delicate black tie-front camisole shirt, which featured lace detailing along the neckline and hem for an extra feminine touch. She added pieces fit for a rock star in the form of a sleek black leather trench coat, matching knee-high boots with a tall black heel, her favorite oversize silver hoop earrings, and—channeling her inner Matrix character—black rectangular shades. Taking a page out of Demi Moore’s book, she added another adorable accessory—objectively the best of all—her black puppy dog, whom she carried in her arm. To help her brave the elements, Shayk also toted a small clear umbrella adorned with white cherries, courtesy of six-year-old daughter Lea De Seine (who appears to have inherited her mom’s great taste).

    Ignat/Bauer-Griffin

    The Russian model shares her daughter with actor Bradley Cooper, whom she separated from in June 2019. Shayk is currently romantically linked to former football player Tom Brady. The couple appear to have an on-again, off-again thing, as they were spotted on a date at Brasserie Fouquet’s in Lower Manhattan earlier this month, which appeared to signify the rekindling of their romance after they reportedly split in October 2023.

    Christina Perrier is a NYC-based freelance writer who covers culture and lifestyle topics. When she’s not writing, she’s probably watching TikToks of dogs, experimenting in the kitchen, or curling up with a book.
