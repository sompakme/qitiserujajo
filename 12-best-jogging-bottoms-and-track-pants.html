    & Other Stories' heavy-duty joggers epitomise the retro trend we've been talking about. Cut from organic cotton-jersey, they have a generous balloon leg that tapers to an elasticated cuff. We've been coveting this grey set for the best part of a year, but now there are even more colour ways (Frankie Shop-inspired muted lavender, taupe and mint tones) to tempt us.

    Oversized Drawstring Trousers, £55, & Other Stories

    Wales Bonner

    .

    SHOP NOW

    Before the 'PE' jogger took over, it was all about the athleisure signifiers – sporty side stripes, sewn-in creases and tri-colour hues. Wales Bonner's track pants tick all these boxes, but swaps nylon webbing for crochet to create a relaxed feel.

    Crochet-striped cotton-jersey joggers, £465, Selfridges

    The Frankie Shop

    .

    SHOP NOW

    Back in 2019, before the pandemic, everyone was cinching The Frankie Shop's Linda boilersuit with strappy sandals. Now, the brand is back with these comfy ankle-cuff joggers so you can re-create the trend with minimal fuss.

    French cotton-terry track pants, £120, Net-a-Porter

    Chinti & Parker

    .

    SHOP NOW

    Chinti & Parker's new tracksuit-inspired pieces have all the luxury of its traditional loungewear but with a sporty twist. These wide-leg joggers are made from a grey wool-cashmere blend and striped in lilac down each side. Pair yours with the matching sweater and ground with sliders when you're WFH.

    Wool-cashmere pants, £195, Chinti & Parker

    Rotate Birger Christensen

    .

    SHOP NOW

    If 2020 was all about The Frankie Shop sweats, now its time for Rotate Sunday to shine – that's the capsule loungewear collection from Jeanette Madsen and Thora Valdimars's typically glam brand. The pair proved, true to form, at Copenhagen Fashion Week that anything can be elevated with a pair of heels.

    Logo cotton jersey trackpants, £105, My Theresa

    SHOP NOW

    Khloe Kardashian's brand Good American has just launched some great loungewear options. Band on trend, these heather-grey joggers are made in the USA from cotton-jersey and cut for a voluminous fit.

    Mid-rise cotton-jersey jogging bottoms, £85, Selfridges

    Loewe

    .

    SHOP NOW

    Trust Jonathan Anderson to give classic sportswear a bohemian touch for Loewe. These joggers may look classic upon first glance with their striped trims, but the open-weave jersey and tasseled drawstring ties keep things feeling relaxed. Pair yours with the brand's matching logo-embroidered hoodie.

    Striped cotton-jersey track pants, £495, Net-a-Porter

    Off-White

    .

    SHOP NOW

    Virgil Abloh was nailing loungewear long before it was a womenswear mainstay. Cut from jersey-twill in a wide-leg shape, these Off-White joggers are finished with sewn-in creases and quilted detailing. The degradé dye, which fades from fuchsia to white, looks even more striking when paired with the matching sweatshirt.

    Tie-dye cotton trackpants, £409, MyTheresa

    The most luxurious of loungewear is always made from cashmere – it offers comfort with a touch of elegance when you're working from home. Burberry's camel tapered joggers are knitted with a hint of stretch and embroidered with the brand's monogram logo at the hip.

    Monogram Cashmere-blend Trackpants, £750, Net-a-Porter

    Fenty

    .

    SHOP NOW

    Rihanna's LVMH label may be shuttering, but you can still shop the brand's impressive loungewear collection for this season. These tracksuit bottoms are made from a technical stretch-jersey that feels luxer than cotton – the draped detailing and scarlet-red hue further elevate them further. Wear yours now with the matching jacket, then try heels and cashmere sweater post-lockdown.

    Cashmere-blend joggers, £437, Stella McCartney at MyTheresa
