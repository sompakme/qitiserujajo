    There is heavy police presence at the Lagos and Abuja chapters of COZA church to prevent protest against the senior pastor of the church, Pastor Biodun Fatoyinbo, over allegation of molestation of Busola Dokolo, the wife of popular singer, Timi Dakolo.

    Legit.ng reports that the protest was organised by #churchtoomovement as advocacy against Biodun Fatoyinbo's molestation allegations.

    At the centre of the protest, the COZA members in Abuja formed human barricade to prevent protesters from proceeding to the front of their church.
