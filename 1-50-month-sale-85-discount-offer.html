    Starz is regularly $9.99 per month, and the new streaming deal saves you 85 percent off your first month of service. This is the best Starz offer we’re seeing online and the cheapest Starz subscription price since Amazon had the streamer discounted to 99 cents for Prime Day.

    Starz is the streaming service you need to add to your subscription list, home to popular shows and film franchises including BMF, Power Book III: Raising Kanan, Outlander, John Wick, Spider-Man: No Way Home, Becoming Elizabeth, P-Valley and more. Subscribers also get access to Starz’s lineup of more than 13 live channels and original TV series such as Courteney Cox’s Shining Vale, Julia Roberts’ Gaslit, the Power Book franchise, Party Down, Minx, the Serpent Queen and more.

    What we love: Starz lets you stream on up to four devices at the same time, with unlimited HD streaming and downloads.
