    24-year-old Chloe Bailey has been facing a ton of flack lately. Not only for collaborating with Chris Brown on her new album, but also for its low first-week sales numbers. During a recent performance on her In Pieces tour, she made it known that she's heard the haters loud and clear. Still, she plans to keep doing what makes her happy, regardless of anyone else's opinion. "F**k what the f**k everybody gotta say," the Atlanta native told her audience last week.

    Bailey kicked off the concerts in support of her album in Chicago on April 11. Following that came another show in Detroit two days later. She was expected to cross the border to entertain audiences in Toronto on the 14th. However, she ultimately had to cancel the show. Thankfully, the "Have Mercy" hitmaker isn't letting that get her down. She seems mighty prepared to put on an iconic performance in Boston tonight (April 17). Earlier this morning, her Instagram feed lit up with a singular black and white image showcasing her bootylicious behind in her stage costume and a pair of fishnet tights.
