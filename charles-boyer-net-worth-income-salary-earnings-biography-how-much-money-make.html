In romantic dramas like as The Garden of Allah (1936), Algiers (1938), and Love Affair (1939), he gave some of the most unforgettable performances of the period (1939). In the 1944 mystery film Gaslight, he played another well-known part. He was nominated for four Academy Awards for Best Actor.
