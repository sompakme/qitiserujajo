    Chase’s absence from the show was due to a tragic accident that occurred on July 4, 2017. The severity of the injury required immediate medical attention and a series of surgeries.

    Despite the life-altering injury, Chase demonstrated remarkable resilience. After a period of recovery, he returned to Fixer to Fabulous, sporting an eye patch. His comeback was met with support and admiration from fans who were inspired by his strength and determination.

    Chase’s Role on the Show Post-Accident
