    A young female student identified as Abigail was swept off her feet by a kind stranger who surprisingly showed up at her school.

    The man, a content creator, accosted Abigail where she was waiting for a bus and gifted her a new car and N411k cash.

    Abigail had thought she was being kidnapped

    In a video capturing the sweet moment shared on ThatWasEpic on Facebook, the kind man had set out to gift 3 cars to 3 lucky students without rides of their own.
