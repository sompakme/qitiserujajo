    18-Inch Sweetheart Locket in Sterling Silver

    There’s something so timeless and nostalgic about a heart-shaped locket. I always hoped to get one from a significant other one day but until then, I’m snagging one of these 18-inch sterling silver styles for my Mom — and maybe myself. The personal element of being able to add photos of my siblings and me inside will definitely warrant happy tears. One reviewer praised its “excellent” quality, and another shopper was so impressed they ordered three more as gifts. 

    7-Inch Infinity Chain Bracelet in Sterling Silver

    For my galentines this year, I’m gifting this delicate infinity chain bracelet. It’s the perfect ‘adult’ friendship bracelet for us to match. The now-$45 piece features a sterling silver infinity symbol connected to a silver cable chain that’s simple enough to coordinate with their other go-to jewelry. One shopper compared it to a popular designer brand’s quality, calling the nearly identical style “absolutely beautiful.” 

    Double Freshwater Cultured Pearl Earrings

    Call me old-fashioned, but I’ve always been a fan of pearls over diamonds and gemstones. I’ll be snagging these understated earrings for 50 percent off that’ll go with just about everything in my wardrobe. And at $65, I might just have to snag a pair for my Mom since she’s also a fan of freshwater pearls. The studs feature a sterling silver post and push back, which won’t irritate my sensitive ears. 

    Petite Garnet Oval Bracelet in Sterling Silver

    My future sister-in-law can be picky when it comes to accessories, but she always has a soft spot for bracelets. This sterling silver piece is thin enough that it won’t look clunky on her small wrists, and it features garnet gemstones for a touch of color. The bracelet is “light to wear and delicate,” per one shopper, and “looks more expensive than it costs,” according to another.
