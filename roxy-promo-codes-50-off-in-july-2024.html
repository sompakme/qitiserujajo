    FAQs

    How do I get a first order discount at Roxy?

    When you sign up for the Roxy newsletter, you will get 20% off your first purchase. As soon as you go to the Roxy website, an email sign-up box should appear. Simply type your preferred email address and click ‘Sign Up’ to be sent your unique promo code.  

    Does Roxy have free shipping?

    When you sign up for the Roxy Girl Club loyalty program, you can get free shipping on all your orders. Standard shipping usually costs $7.95 without the loyalty program. Your order should arrive within 2-4 business days.  

    Can I get a student discount at Roxy?

    At Roxy, students can enjoy a 15% off discount. Simply verify your student status via UNiDAYS. You’ll then be able to access your promo code and shop for beachwear, swimsuits, and clothing for less.  

    When does Roxy have sales?

    Roxy has sales throughout the year from its dedicated ‘sale’ page. We’ve previously seen discounts of up to 70% off in the Roxy sales. Simply go to the Roxy website and choose the ‘sale’ tab. You can then filter your results to suit your budget and shopping needs.  

    What is the return policy at Roxy?

    If you would like to, you can return it to Roxy within 30 days of purchase. Your item should be returned in its original condition and with any tags attached. Any final sale items cannot be returned. If you sign up for Roxy Girl Club, you will get free returns. Non-members will need to pay a return fee of $6.95, which will be deducted from your refund total.
