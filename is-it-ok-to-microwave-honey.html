    How do you soften honey in the microwave?

    First, transfer your honey to a microwave safe glass jar. Then, place it in the microwave for 30 seconds. Stir the honey, and if it is still hard, microwave again for an additional 15 seconds. Be very careful to NOT let it come to a boil.

    How long do you microwave honey?

    Another way to decrystallize honey is to place the honey in a microwave-safe container, with the lid removed. Then, microwave the honey over medium power for 30 seconds at a time, stirring in between microwaving sessions. What is this? Continue until it has been decrystallized.

    Can you heat crystallized honey?

    If your honey crystallizes, simply place the honey jar in warm water and stir until the crystals dissolve. Or, place the honey in a microwave-safe container with the lid off and microwave, stirring every 30 seconds, until the crystals dissolve. Be careful not to boil or scorch the honey.
