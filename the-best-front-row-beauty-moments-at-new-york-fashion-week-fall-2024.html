    01 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Fashion Month is, of course, all about the collections that come down the runway. But it's not just the models serving up inspiration â€” it's also the stars studding the front row. Whether headed past the cameras en route to their seat or perched in front of the catwalk, attendees lent their own own style and above-the-neck attitude to the show.

    Ahead, find some of our favorite front looks so far and come back throughout the week to see what else catches our eye.

    Ashlee Simpson

    Simpson is letting her eyes do all the work with her baby blues rimmed in a dark black eyeliner. The rest of her look is kept neutral with just a hint of blush and a pinky nude lip.

    Emira D'Spain

    Christian Siriano's collection was all about sparkle and metallics, so this look from model Emira D'Spain was a perfect fit. It features a striking smoked-out cat eye, pretty pink tones on the lips and cheeks, and a tons of crystals all over her face.

    Brooke Shields

    There's something oh-so-striking about a bold eye look behind a pair of glasses. Brooke Shields is wearing a moody smoky eye punctuated by a strip of bold lashes. She paired the darker eye look with a fun peachy-pink lip and cheek.

    Greivy

    Content creator Greivy went bright and shimmery with her sparkly blue lids, leaning heavily into the 2024 blue beauty trend. The shadow is most concentrated under the crease and then beautifully blended up to just below the brows. She paired the eyes with a warm peachy blush and a high-shine lip gloss.

    Alicia Silverstone

    To stay in the red color family of her dress, actress Alicia Silverstone kept her makeup mauvey. She wears the shade on her lids, cheeks, and lips for a monochromatic moment.

    Sophia Bush

    Although Sophia Bush is rocking a charcoal smoky eye, she wore a champagne shimmer under her crease for a touch of gleam. She opted to go wingless for her eyeliner and added drama with fluffy lashes. The rest of the look is clean and neutral with a touch of blush and a peachy-beige glossy lip.
