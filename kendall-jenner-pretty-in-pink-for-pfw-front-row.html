    Kendall Jenner pretty in pink for PFW front row

    Kendall Jenner took a break from the catwalks at Paris Fashion Week on Tuesday to take her place on the front row. The 19-year-old showed off her girlie side in a chic pink ensemble as she watched the Spring/Summer 2016 presentation.

    10 Aug 2024, 00:00

      Kendall Jenner took a break from the catwalks at Paris Fashion Week on Tuesday to take her place on the front row. The 19-year-old showed off her girlie side in a chic pink ensemble as she watched the Spring/Summer 2016 presentation.

      Kendall Jenner turned heads in a pretty pink outfit

      Thestar gave fans a glimpse of her incredible figure in a pink metallic miniskirt paired with a pale pink blouse with floral embroidered detailing. Showing off her style credentials, Kendall accessorised her stylish ensemble with a pair of metallic silver lace-up boots, which matched her silver nail polish.

      The young model kept her hair and beauty looks simple but elegant, sweeping her glossy black locks up into a slightly dishevelled high ponytail, and opting for barely-there make-up for a fresh-faced style.

      Just a few hours earlier, Kendall was turning heads at the opulent Grand Palais as she walked in Chanel's airline-themed Spring/Summer 2016 runway show.

      Kendall walked in Chanel's airline-themed Spring/Summer 2016 show

      The reality TV star rocked two dazzling ensembles from Karl Lagerfeld's designs – a chic blue tweed two-piece, as well as a cropped black-and-white tweed jacket paired with a large black leather skirt and trousers.

      Kendall, who has stormed the runways for major designers including Balmain, Michael Kors and Diane von Furstenberg, recently was revealed to have made' list of the world's highest-earning models for the first time, becoming the youngest model on the list.

      With a pay packet of £2.5million last year, the American beauty has joined the likes of Gisele Bündchen, Karlie Kloss, Doutzen Kroes, Cara Delevingne and Kate Moss in the rankings.

      It's not just the catwalks which have seen Kendall conquer the industry – she has bagged major campaigns including Balmain and Calvin Klein, as well as signing her first major beauty contract with cosmetics giant Estée Lauder.
