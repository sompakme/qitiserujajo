    FAQs

    What shipping options does Champs offer?

    Standard shipping to the US will cost up to $9.98 and will be delivered within 3-5 business days. Alternatively, expedited shipping will cost up to $15.97 and will be delivered within 3-4.

    Can I shop the Champs sale?

    Champs runs sale events during seasonal periods such as Christmas and Black Friday, but you can also shop the Champs sale all year long by heading to the 'Sale' page of the Champs website. A Champs store coupon can occasionally be used online as well so check out the terms and conditions to check what your Champs discount code is eligible for.

    How can I get a Champs student discount?

    Students can confirm their student ID when they sign into Student Beans. You’ll then be able to get 20% off your Champs online order over $99. All you need to do is verify your student status to access this Champs Sports coupon. 

    Can I sign up for the Champs email newsletter?

    Yes - simply type your email address into the sign-up box at the footer of the Champs website. This will mean that you're kept up to date about the latest Champs news, exclusive offers, sale events, product launches, and loads more.  

    Does Champs have military discounts?

     Yes. Champs offers a 10% military discount on their online or in-store orders. To access this discount, log into SheerID to confirm your military status. You can then enter your exclusive military code at the checkout.  

    What is the Champs returns policy?

    If you'd like to return your Champs purchase, you have up to 45 days from the original shipment date to send your items back. All items must be in their original packaging and with all the labels and tags in place. Please note that your refund can take up to 10 working days to arrive in your account.
