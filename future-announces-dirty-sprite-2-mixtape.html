    20 Aug 2024, 00:00

    There isn't a whole lot of consensus in the Future mixtape canon (namely because there are so many, and pretty much all of them are good), but Dirty Sprite is generally agreed upon as a strong contender for the Atlanta rapper's best non-album work. Early fans will be excited to hear the news that Fewtch is working on a follow-up to the beloved project, announcing the news on Twitter today.

    The new tape comes just a few months after Future hit us with both the menacing Metro Boomin-produced Monster, and the bright, poppy Beast Mode. We're still waiting on Ape Shit, his upcoming tape with Mike WiLL, so we're excited to learn who he'll he collaborating with on DS2. The first installment featured beats from Sonny Digital, DJ Spinz, and Nard & B, as well as WiLL and Zaytoven, so we should expect a good range of producers.
