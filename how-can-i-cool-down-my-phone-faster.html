    How to cool an overheated phone

  • Turn the phone off.

  • Take your phone out of its case, if you're using one.
  • Remove the charging cable if it's plugged in.
  • Place your phone in your freezer; just don't leave it in there too long.

    Whatever you do, don't put an overheated phone in the refrigerator or freezer. Although both iPhones and Androids are designed to be used at temperatures as low as 32 degrees Fahrenheit, huge swings in temperature and exposure to moisture can damage your phone beyond repair.

    • What temperature is bad for phone battery?

    The normal range for prolonged battery life is 62 to 72 degrees Fahrenheit. High temperatures over 95 degrees (35 degrees Celcius) can decrease battery life and temperatures lower than 50 degrees (10 degrees Celcius) can permanently shorten battery life.
