    For fans of action, thriller, and horror, alien movies provide these three factors in great heaps. The best alien movies today are filled with these three factors and are fantastic for anyone in the mood for an adrenaline rush.

    The question as to whether aliens and other life forms exist in the universe have plagued humans for many years. Through the years, many people have claimed to have seen UFOs, and more and more people are convinced that they exist.

    While some are convinced that they exist, others such as Edward Snowden claim that extraterrestrials are not real or, rather, if they exist, they have not yet made contact with humans.

    Best alien movies today

    Human fascination with space and extraterrestrial beings has brought about some of the best alien movies from the creative minds in Hollywood.

    They explore different factors: if aliens are friendly beings, if they can control minds. They delve even further into creepy subjects such as body snatching and using people as food.

    Our list of best alien movies includes some of the highest-rated films that explore the 'what ifs' of human-alien interactions.

    15. Under the Skin

    IMDb score - 6.3/10

    • Release date: 14th March 2014 (UK)
    • Director: Jonathan Glazer
    • Studio: A24
    • Starring: Scarlett Johansson, Jeremy McWilliams, Lynsey Taylor Mackay

    In this alien invasion movie, Scarlett Johansson plays the part of a beautiful woman who has been taken over by an alien. The nameless creature is on a mission to earth to lure and kidnap men so that they may be used as food in another dimension.

    In the course of the film, the creature inside Scarlett's body learns more about humans and humanity and eventually starts rejecting its mission. It should be noted that the movie does not have a lot of dialogue in it.

    Despite this, it has a very chilling and eerie feel and look that gets under the viewer's skin. This gives it a spot on our best alien movies list.

    14. Dark Skies

    IMDb score - 6.3/10

    • Release date: 22nd February 2013
    • Director: Scott Stewart
    • Studio: The Weinstein Company
    • Starring: Keri Russell, Jake Brennan, Josh Hamilton

    Dark Skies is the definition of what alien horror movies are. The film follows the Barrett family, who have fallen on hard times after the husband and father, Daniel, loses his job.

    His wife, Lacy, now has to provide for the family as Daniel stays at home with their two young sons. In the midst of all this, things start going wrong when the family realizes that someone or something is breaking into their home, rearranging their kitchen, and standing over them as they sleep.

    Daniel and Lacy now have the daunting task of protecting their children, who they now believe the unknown beings are after. The psychological horror aspect of the movie makes Dark Skies one of the best alien movies today.

    13. Venom

    IMDb score - 6.7/10

    • Release date: 5th October 2018
    • Director: Ruben Fleischer
    • Studio: Columbia Pictures
    • Starring: Tom Hardy, Michelle Williams, Riz Ahmed

    The movie follows journalist Eddie Brock who, after hearing rumours of human experiments at The Life Foundation, goes on a mission to expose the company and its CEO, Carlton Drake.

    During his investigation, he ends up possessed by one of the alien life forms, a symbiote that Carlton's company has been using on human test subjects.

    From here on, Eddie and the symbiote have to learn how to co-exist in the same body as they fight to protect themselves from Carlton Drake and the earth from an oncoming symbiote army.

    Which is the best alien movie?

    In most best alien movies list, The Thing will often take the number one spot as the best alien movie ever. However, in our case, we believe that Alien is the best alien movie ever made.

    1. Alien

    IMDb score - 8.4/10

    • Release date: 22nd June 1979 (USA)
    • Director: Ridley Scott
    • Studio: 20th Century Fox
    • Starring: Sigourney Weaver, Tom Skerritt, John Hurt

    Alien celebrated it's 40th anniversary just last year (2019). Despite being released almost 41 years ago, it beats all others as number one in the best alien movies list.

    Not only is it downright scary but the hopelessness experienced by the characters as they fight for their lives is exactly what horror movies should strive for

    Alien follows a seven-member crew in a commercial space tug called Nostromo. During their trip back home, they are woken up by a distress signal from a nearby ship. The team follows protocol and decides to investigate.

    They all land on the moon to discover too late that it was not a ship in distress but an alien ship emitting a warning signal to all those around. The astronauts must now fight for their lives and their chance to make it back home safely.
