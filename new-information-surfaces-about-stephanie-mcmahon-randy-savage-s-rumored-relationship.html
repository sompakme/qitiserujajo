    New Information Surfaces About Stephanie McMahon &amp; Randy Savages Rumored Relationship

    Stephanie McMahon is married to Triple H. They have 3 daughters and are the biggest power couple in WWE. There is also a dark pro wrestling rumor that Stephanie McMahon had a relationship with Macho Man Randy Savage back in the day.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Stephanie McMahon is married to Triple H. They have 3 daughters and are the biggest power couple in WWE. There is also a dark pro wrestling rumor that Stephanie McMahon had a relationship with “Macho Man” Randy Savage back in the day.

    This rumor has been addressed in the past. Hannibal stated during a recent interview with Gorgeous George that Lanny Poffo, Savage’s brother told him “off the record” what really happened. This sparked a very interesting revelation from George.

    Gorgeous George used to spend personal time with Randy Savage. She told Hannibal TV that he disclosed to her what really happened with Stephanie McMahon.

    George was coy about the story, and the look on her face really explained the context she was alluding to. Feel free to check out the video below to see how she said what she said. You can read what she said here:

    “Oh, he told me. I think he didn’t mean to tell me that and the next morning he asked me ‘did I tell you anything last night at the beach?’ I said, ‘You sure did, you know exactly what you said,’ and he said, ‘sh*t’ — you know what I mean because it was kinda like the truth drug. Once he got on that he started telling me everything and it is what it is, you know he knows she knows what it all was.”

    Gorgeous George did not reveal what Randy Savage told her on the beach that night. Since the Macho Man is sadly no longer with us he will never tell all. Odds are Stephanie McMahon isn’t going to spill the beans either.

    Savage apparently told more than one person what happened and Hannibal allegedly knows via Savage’s brother Lanny Poffo. Perhaps one of the people who Randy Savage told while on his “truth drug” will come forward and clear up this long-standing urban legend in pro wrestling.
