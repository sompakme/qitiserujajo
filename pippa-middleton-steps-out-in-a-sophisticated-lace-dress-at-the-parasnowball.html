    Pippa Middleton wore an elegant lace adorned dress to the Para Snow Ball at the Hurlingham Club on Thursday evening

      Pippa Middleton looked stunning in an elegant ensemble at the ParaSnowBall at the Hurlingham Club in London. Dressed in a floor-length lace dress that showcased her enviable slim physique, the 33-year old channelled a sophisticated look as she attended the event. She elevated her get-up with a pair of statement sandals and carried a stylish metalic silver clutch bag.

      Pippa looked beautiful as she stepped out to attend the charity event.  Copyright: Camera Press/ Les Wilson

      She wore her lustrous chocolate-brown hair down in a middle parting and her skin looked suitably radiant. The British beauty opted for a natural make-up look which offset her beautiful outfit perfectly and accentuated her feminine features. Her flawless base was subtly sculpted and enlivened with a generous sweep of rose-toned blush.

      Eye-wise, Pippa worked a grey shadow that was blended skilfully across her lids, liquid liner and lots of volumising mascara. Her sophisticated look was completed with a subtle pink lipstick.

      The event, of which Pippa is a sponsor, celebrates the ongoing achievements of the athletes and the incredible sporting opportunities that Disability Snowsport UK offers to anyone with a disability. Attendees are treated to an evening of fine dining and entertainment along with a number of fundraising activities, and the dress code is black tie.

      The ParaSnowBall is thought to be the only event Pippa and James will attend before they tie the knot on 20th May at St Mark's Church in Englefield. There are bound to be countless high fashion figures playing a part in the couple's big day but they have kept completely schtum on the topic of the wedding dress.
