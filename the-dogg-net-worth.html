    About

    Namibian Rapper who has become a major force in the world of the house music subgenre known as kwaito. Albums like 2006's Introducing TeeDee and 2009's This is My Time have made him one of Zambia's best-selling Musicians. 

    Before Fame

    He grew up in the village of Onayena and studied at the University of Namibia for one year. He was a member of the group Omalaeti O'Swapo before going solo in 2004.
