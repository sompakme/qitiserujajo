    John MacArthur’s Early Life

    John Fullerton MacArthur Jr. was born on June 19, 1939, in Los Angeles, California. He is the third generation of his family to enter the ministry. His father, Dr. Jack MacArthur, was a well-known preacher and the president of a Christian college in Los Angeles. John MacArthur graduated from Talbot Theological Seminary with a Master of Divinity degree in 1964.

    Ministry and Teaching Career
