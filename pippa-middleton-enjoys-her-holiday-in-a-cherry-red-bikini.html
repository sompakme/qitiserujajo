    Pippa Middleton has swapped the winter chill for some fun in the sun in St. Barts.

    The younger sister of the Princess of Wales was spotted during her holiday hanging out at a beach in a cherry red triangle bikini. The swimsuit included twisted straps and gold hardware. She accessorized with oversized cat-eye sunglasses with thick tortoiseshell frames, plus a few gold rings.

    Pippa's husband, James Matthews, and their two children, four-year-old Arthur and one-year-old Grace, were also seen at the beach enjoying some quality family time.

    MEGA

    James Middleton, Princess Kate and Pippa's brother, recently opened up about his close bond with his siblings. In an essay published by the Sunday Times last month, James said his sisters helped him overcome the grief of losing his beloved dog, a 15-year-old cocker spaniel named Ella.

    "She was next to me through my darkest times, her head in my lap. Winston Churchill would complain that he was followed by the 'black dog' of depression. I had my own black dog but she was my saving grace," James wrote of his certified therapy pet. "Everybody knew how much she meant to me and there was a day they feared the most: the day Ella was no longer in my life. She meant something to all of them too."

    When Ella's final day came, James wrote, "All the family came to say their goodbyes. I can't speak for them but I believe she influenced their lives too. Lupo, Catherine and William's dog, was one of Ella's puppies. My sister Pippa and her husband have a pup from Ella, as do other family members."

    The sisters also attended therapy with James.

    "My sisters came to some of my therapy sessions. They've always been there during difficult times and they were at my side during the hardest of times as well. For that, I am forever grateful," he said.
