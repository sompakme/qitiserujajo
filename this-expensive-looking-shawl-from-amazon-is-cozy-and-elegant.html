    We’ve all been dying to live our lives as if we’re in a Nancy Meyers movie. You know the vibe: coastal grandma, swanning about in light neutrals and draped in soft, cozy fabrics. However, this type of clothing can be expensive. (After all, some of Meyers’ most popular movies take place in locales like The Hamptons and Napa.) While it’s not impossible to get the look for less, it does take some searching, and I’m pleased to announce I’ve found a wardrobe staple that evokes the mood without breaking the bank. 

    The Moss Rose Shawl Wrap is a poncho-style wrap sweater that’ll make winter dressing more elegant. Made from a thick viscose blend fabric, it’s super soft yet sturdy to keep you warm and give your outfit some weight. It comes in 35 styles and colors, ranging from classic checkered patterns to neutrals, some of which are even reversible for double the wear.

    I’m usually pretty weary of one size fits most sizing, but I was surprised at how nicely the shawl fit on my 5-foot-8 frame over a tissue turtleneck. My petite mother even tried it on and liked the fit on her body, too. It can be worn in several ways: open front like a shawl, with a belt, or tucked into a sewn-on tab on the front of the poncho. Tucked in is my favorite way of wearing it, as it’s easy to slide in and out depending on how hot or cold I am, and it looks stylish with very little effort. Plus, for as low as $33 in select colors, the shawl is high-quality; it’s thick and looks more expensive than it costs.

    The Moss Rose Shawl Wrap is an Amazon best-seller for a reason, with more than 3,400 shoppers singing its praises. One reviewer called it “beautiful, vibrant, soft, [and] warm.” “I'll be buying more of these for myself and as gifts,” they said. Another person called it “effortlessly chic” and said that it’s “great for air travel as it doubles as a throw.” “The band makes it stay in place better than most shawls without pinning,” they added.

    If you need a good layering piece this winter at a price point that would please any coastal grandmother, grab the Moss Rose Shawl Wrap at Amazon.
