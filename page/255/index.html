Was Van Gogh Left Handed?

06 Jul 2024, 00:00

Van Gogh was right-handed. There is no evidence or documentation to suggest that he was left-handed. Throughout his life, Van Gogh primarily used his right hand for writing, drawing, and painting. His letters and sketches demonstrate a consistent right-handed script and stroke patterns. Additionally, the majority of his paintings appear to reflect a right-handed painting technique, with brushstrokes executed from left to right.However, it is intriguing to consider the impact that being left-handed might have had on Van Gogh's artistic expression.
