01 Aug 2024, 00:00

The difference in widths is only 2% and can be discounted, but the stitch height of garter is 30% smaller than for stockinette. Does garter stitch knit up bigger than stocking stitch? Garter stitch (knit every row) uses more yarn than stockinette stitch (knit 1 row, purl 1 row) because it is not as tall as stockinette stitch. Garter stitch also uses more yarn than lace knitting. Lace tends to be open and airy so the yarn will go a lot further than knitting in garter stitch.
