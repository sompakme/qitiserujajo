    Dorothy Mathe shared a heartwarming social media post on Wednesday, March 4, that gave South Africans the feels.

    Dorothy revealed that her grandmother celebrated a fantastic milestone on Wednesday - it was her 100th birthday.

    She took to Facebook and shared some delightful pics of their festivities in the #ImStaying group.

    Legit.ng noted the amazing reaction as thousands of social media users gave the post the stamp of approval.

    More than 1500 comments were shared wishing the wise grandmother a happy birthday.
