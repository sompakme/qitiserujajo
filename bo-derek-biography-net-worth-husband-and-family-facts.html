    Bo Derek is a multi-talented American model, actress, and film producer who rose to prominence in the 80s. Bo was considered one of the most famous sex symbols of the 80s; she worked as a swimsuit and Playboy model. Interestingly, she is also notable for being the former wife of John Derek who was a renowned American actor and director. Her ex-husband was known for his work on Tarzan and Bolero which helped catapult Derek into the limelight. Learn more about the stunning actress below.

    Biography of Bo Derek

    The star actress was born and named Mary CathleenCollins on November 20, 1956. She was raised in Long Beach, California by her parents Paul Collins and wife Norma Collins. Her father, Paul, worked as an executive at Hobie Cat while her mother was a makeup artist and hairstylist at Ann-Margret. When she was very young, her parents divorced and her mother was linked to an American stuntman Bobby Bass who became Derek’s stepfather.

    Growing up, Bo enrolled in Narbonne High School where she spent a few years before transferring to George S. Patton High School to complete her high school education. Sadly, young Bo and the budding model didn’t finish high school, but she dropped out to pursue her acting career at the age of 16.

    Her acting career and rise to prominence

    Becoming an actress, Bo made her first appearance in the 1977 film titled Orca. There she played the role of Annie and after that she starred in the 1979 movie titled 10 for which she got the spotlight. Bo was also nominated for a Golden Globe Award for her outstanding performance in 10. Along with Anthony Hopkins, Bo starred in the comedy film titled A Change of Seasons in 1980.

    The incredible actress got her breakthrough in 1981 when she portrayed the role of Jane Parker in the hit movie titled Tarzan the Ape. Bo won a Golden Raspberry Award for Worst Actress in Film. She also received the same award for her next two films, including Bolero in 1984 and Ghosts Can Do It in 1990.

    During the ’90s, Bo Derek made occasional appearances in films such as Christina Ford, Wind Over Water, Hot Chocolate, and Beverly Barish-Burns Callahan, among others. Thereafter, she featured in a handful of films including 5 Weddings, Highland Park, Crusader, Queen of Swords, Family Law, 7th Heaven, The Last Sharkando: It’s About Time and many more.

    Net Worth: How much is Bo Derek worth?

    The stunning actress has recorded huge successes since the start of her acting career. She has accumulated a lot of wealth from both her acting career as well as her career as a model and film producer. His net worth is currently pegged at $50 million. She earns her net worth through her acting work and other business pursuits.

    Who is her husband?

    Bo Derek first fell in love with John Derekshe was sixteen years old. At the time, John was 30 years older than Bo and he was married to an actress named Linda Evans. However, it didn’t take long for John to divorce his wife so he could be with Bo. Despite the fact that Bo was underage, the duo moved to Germany where they got married immediately after her 18th birthday. They exchanged their marital vows at a small wedding held in 1976.

    Her husband John was a famous director, actor and photographer. He helped Bo find his footing in the film industry early in his career. John has directed several films including Nightmare in the Sun, Once Before I Die, Tarzan, The Ape Man and many more. As an actor, John has also appeared in numerous films such as Run for Cover, Prisoner of the Volga, and Thieves of Sherwood Forest, among others. He also worked as a photographer for Bo as well as his two other wives in Playboy Magazine

    Bo and John Derek’s marriage flourished for over 22 years until his death in 1998. Although they had no children, they were certainly lifelong partners. John Derek is believed to have died of a heart attack in May 1998 and was 71 at the time of his death.

    However, two years after her death, Bo started another relationship with an actor named John Corbett and the couple have lived together in California to this day. Their relationship has lasted for over sixteen years. Although they weren’t married yet, the couple went on to live happily ever after.
