    A Troubled Love Life

    Darius McCrary, the well-known actor famous for his role as Eddie Winslow on the 90s sitcom Family Matters, finds himself in the midst of a tumultuous relationship once again. Recently, he sought a restraining order against his ex-girlfriend, alleging that she physically attacked him and damaged his car’s windows with a lead pipe.

    The Denied Restraining Order

    At first, the court denied the temporary restraining order. The reason? McCrary failed to appear on the scheduled hearing date. However, he later explained that he felt “displaced” and had to escape from his ex’s presence. In court documents, McCrary revealed that he is “emotionally scarred” and unable to focus on his work or daily activities due to fear. Shockingly, he claims that the abuse he endured included physical, verbal, and mental torment, and he suspects the use of narcotics in these abusive acts.

    Past Marital Struggles

    Darius McCrary’s love life has been far from smooth sailing, marked by multiple relationships and marriages that ended in turmoil. Let’s take a closer look at his tumultuous romantic history.

    A Quick Marriage and Divorce

    McCrary’s first romantic endeavor was with Juliette Vann, a performer from Las Vegas. They began dating in 2005 and decided to tie the knot just four months later. Unfortunately, their marital bliss was short-lived, and they parted ways in 2006, ending their marriage.

    Engagement Turned Sour

    In 2008, McCrary entered into a relationship with novelist Karrine Steffans. Within three months, they got engaged and were married in 200Regrettably, their marriage was marred by allegations of domestic and physical abuse, leading to their divorce in 2011.

    Troubled Marriage to a Basketball Player

    In 2014, McCrary married Tammy Brawner, a basketball player. However, their union quickly soured, with Brawner accusing the actor of domestic abuse. In her divorce petition to the Los Angeles Superior Court, she described him as “violent, destructive, and emotionally unstable.” She further alleged that he neglected their child and struggled with substance addiction. McCrary’s attorney vehemently denied these allegations, labeling Brawner as a “predator.” Despite their divorce in 2019, the custody battle over their child raged on, ultimately granting Brawner full custody and allowing McCrary limited supervised visitation.

    An Engagement Shrouded in Mystery

    Despite his rocky romantic past, McCrary recently revealed that he is engaged once again. However, the identity of his fiancée remains a well-guarded secret. It remains to be seen whether this new chapter in McCrary’s love life will follow the same tumultuous pattern as his previous relationships.

    Darius McCrary’s life, both in love and in court, has been far from ordinary. His recent allegations against his ex-girlfriend only add to the rollercoaster of experiences he has endured. As the legal proceedings unfold, the world watches to see what comes next in this ongoing saga of love, strife, and personal growth.
