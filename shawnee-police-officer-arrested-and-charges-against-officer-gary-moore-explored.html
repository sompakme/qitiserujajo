    Shawnee Police Officer Faces Drug Trafficking Charges

    In a recent incident that has grabbed public attention, Gary Moore, a 50-year-old former Shawnee police officer, was arrested on Friday, November 10, 202The charges against Moore include narcotics trafficking, leading to his dismissal from the Shawnee Police Department. The arrest took place at the Brickhouse Saloon in downtown Shawnee, as reported by Fox23 News.

     Multiple Agencies Collaborate in Investigation

    The Pottawatomie County Sheriff’s Office, the District Attorney’s Office, and the Shawnee Police Department are working closely together to investigate the allegations made against Gary Moore. The authorities have not disclosed the duration of Moore’s possible involvement in the criminal activity, nor have they revealed specific details surrounding his arrest.

     Gary Moore Faces Single Count of Possession with Intent to Distribute

    Following the arrest, Gary Moore is facing a single count of possession with the intent to distribute. He is currently in jail without bond, according to court documents. The investigation leading to Moore’s arrest involved various law enforcement agencies, including the Shawnee Police Department, the district attorney’s office, and the sheriff’s offices of Pottawatomie County and Cleveland County.

    Moore Dismissed from Shawnee Police Department

    Before Moore’s arrest, an investigation was conducted, leading to his dismissal from the Shawnee Police Department.
