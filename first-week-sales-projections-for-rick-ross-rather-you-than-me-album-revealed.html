    First Week Sales Projections For Rick Ross Rather You Than Me Album Revealed

    The early first week sales projections are in for Rick Ross newly-released 9th studio LP Rather You Than Me, and as expected its looking like itll be #2 album of the week behind Drakes More Life of course.

    16 Sep 2024, 00:00

    The early first week sales projections are in for Rick Ross’ newly-released 9th studio LP Rather You Than Me, and as expected it’s looking like it’ll be #2 album of the week behind Drake’s More Life of course.

    According to HDD, Rather You Than Me, which is Rozay’s first release with Epic Records, is currently on track to move anywhere from 100-105K units in the opening week, with 40-50K coming from direct sales. In comparison, Rozay’s last album, Black Market, moved only 65K in its opening week, meaning the MMG boss is looking at nearly a 40K increase in sales this time around.

    To help these numbers out even more though, fans can purchase Rather You Than Me now on iTunes. Stream it here first on HNHH if you haven't done so already.
